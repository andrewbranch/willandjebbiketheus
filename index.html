Jeb Hunt

Jeb Hunt, 31, is an avid cyclist and is currently working on his Masters Degree at Duke Divinity School. He is a graduate of the University of West Florida, with a degree in Communicatin Arts. He enjoys technology (especially Apple) and works as the Communications and Contemporary Worship Arts Director at First United Methodist Church of Pensacola. Jeb has competed in multiple marathons, half-marathons and triathlons and has traveled to more than 24 countries. He looks forward to the adventure of traveling across the USA and meeting new people and places.

Will Bulla

Will Bulla, 21, is an avid cyclist and student at Pensacola State College with a major in Computer Systems Information. Will enjoys technology (especially Android) and works at KMS Telecommunications in Pensacola, Florida. Will is also a talented drummer with the local band BANNER. In preparation for the upcoming journey across America, Will has competed in the Gulf Coast Interstate Relay, the first officially sanctioned USA Cycling bicycle relay, and is currently training for a triathlon.
